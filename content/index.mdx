Welcome! This is a strategy / wiki site for Shiren the Wanderer 2 (Nintendo 64)<br/>
to accompany the English patch created by Ozidual and SharkSnack.

Check the <a href="/shiren-2/guides/faq">FAQ</a> page for the patch download link, instructions, graphic issue fix for emulators, etc.

If you need additional help, feel free to join the <a href="https://discord.gg/5y7UDFc">Shiren the Wanderer</a> Discord server and ask questions.<br/>Feedback is also appreciated. (#shiren-2 channel under Patch Feedback)

This site is a work-in-progress, so some pages may change in time.<br/>Feel free to follow the <a href="https://krikzz.com/forum/index.php?topic=8495">project thread</a> over at the EverDrive Forum.

A significant portion of the information found on this site is hand-translated from the <a href="http://shiren2.lsx3.com/">Japanese wiki</a>.
