Welcome! This is a strategy / wiki site for Shiren the Wanderer 2 (Nintendo 64)<br/>
to accompany the English patch created by ozidual and sharksnack.

The English patch hasn't been released yet, but a playtest version is available for download.<br/>If you're interested in testing, join the <a href="https://discord.gg/5y7UDFc">Shiren the Wanderer</a> Discord server for details.

This site is a work-in-progress, so some pages don't have content yet, and others may change in time.<br/>Feel free to follow the <a href="https://krikzz.com/forum/index.php?topic=8495">project thread</a> over at the EverDrive Forum.

A significant portion of the information found on this site is hand-translated from the <a href="http://shiren2.lsx3.com/">Japanese wiki</a>.
